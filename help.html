LIFE Fellowship is a Christian college fellowship.

With church so far from where students typically live, we need a way to get people there. That's where carpool ministry comes in.

How does this all work?

Everyone who has indicated that they need a ride will be assigned to a certain car. All you have to do is show up to your pick-up spot on time, and someone will pick you up! Ride assignments are always emailed the night before (Thursday/Saturday).

What are LIFE's regular meetings?

- Friday night fellowship at 7:30pm on Fridays (pickup at 6:50pm)
- Sunday School at 9:15am on Sundays (pickup at 8:50am)
- and Sunday worship service at 11:00am on Sundays (no pickups)

We also have weekly small groups, but we typically meet at our respective campuses for these.

Where does LIFE Fellowship meet?

We are affiliated with Chinese Evangelical Church of San Diego, located in Linda Vista, and this is where we gather for all of our regular meetings.

Who's going to drive me?

All of our drivers are fellow students who are kind enough to sacrifice their time and gas to get everyone to church. Make sure to thank them!

When can I request a ride?

You can start requesting rides at 9am on Wednesday.

- The deadline for requesting a ride to Friday night fellowship is Thursday night at 9pm.
- The deadline for requesting a ride to Sunday school & service is Saturday night at 9pm.

What kind of people are in LIFE Fellowship?

LIFE is a group specifically for college students. We have members from UC San Diego, San Diego State University, and Point Loma Nazarene University. Anyone is welcome to check us out!

I still have some questions. Who can I ask?

If you have any questions, feel free to shoot an email to ceclifecarpool@gmail.com, and we'll try to help you out!
