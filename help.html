LIFE Fellowship is a Christian college fellowship.

With church so far from where students typically live, we need a way to get people there. That's where carpool ministry comes in.

Where does LIFE Fellowship meet?

We are affiliated with Chinese Evangelical Church of San Diego, located in Linda Vista, and this is where we gather for all of our regular meetings.

What kind of people are in LIFE Fellowship?

LIFE is a group specifically for college students. We have members from UC San Diego, San Diego State University, and Point Loma Nazarene University.

What are LIFE's regular meetings?

We have Friday night fellowship at 7:30pm on Fridays, Sunday School at 9:15am on Sundays, and Sunday worship service at 11:00am on Sundays at CEC. We also have weekly small groups, but we don't meet at CEC for these.

Who's going to drive me?

All of our drivers are fellow students who are kind enough to sacrifice their time and gas to pick you up. Make sure to thank them!

I still have some questions. Who can I ask?

If you have any questions, feel free to shoot us an email at ceclifecarpool@gmail.com and we'll try to help you out!
